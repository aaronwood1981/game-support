Support

Help with Stone & Tankard Games.

If you run into an issue with Lights Over Harrow Farm or another Stone & Tankard Games release, send a clear description and the details below.

Contact

Email support at stoneandtankardgames@gmail.com.

Please include as much of the following information as you can:

  • Game title
  • Device model
  • iOS or iPadOS version
  • App version
  • A short description of the issue

Before you write

For display, audio, save, or progress issues, it can also help to mention what you were doing right before the problem appeared. A brief step-by-step note is often enough.

Stone & Tankard Games is a solo studio, so replies may not be instant, but support messages are read with care.
