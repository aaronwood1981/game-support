About the Studio

Small games with a strong sense of place.

Stone & Tankard Games is a solo independent studio focused on building approachable mobile games with atmosphere, character, and replayable progression. The goal is not to chase every genre, but to create compact experiences that feel handcrafted and memorable.

What We Make

Interactive fiction, narrative RPGs, cozy merge games, clicker-style progression, mystery adventures, and other event-driven mobile games where choices and discovery matter.
