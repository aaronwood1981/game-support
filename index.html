Studio

Small games with a sense of place.

Stone & Tankard Games is a solo independent studio making atmospheric mobile experiences with a handcrafted feel. The studio is built around readable writing, steady progression, memorable places, and choices that make a world feel remembered.

Each game is designed to be approachable on a phone, but rooted in the older pleasures of stories, maps, mysteries, and paths that invite another look.
