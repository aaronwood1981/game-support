Privacy policy

Privacy at Stone & Tankard Games.

This policy describes how Stone & Tankard Games handles privacy for Lights Over Harrow Farm and future releases.

Last updated: June 30, 2026

Lights Over Harrow Farm

Lights Over Harrow Farm does not collect personal data.

  • No account is required.
  • No analytics are used.
  • No ads are shown.
  • No in-app purchases are included.
  • No third-party tracking is used.
  • No personal data is collected by Stone & Tankard Games through the app.

Local save data

Any save data for Lights Over Harrow Farm stays local on your device. Stone & Tankard Games does not receive, store, or access that save data.

Future games and updates

Future Stone & Tankard Games releases may add features such as ads, analytics, in-app purchases, or online services. If that happens, this privacy policy will be updated to describe what data is involved and how it is handled.

Contact

For privacy questions, email stoneandtankardgames@gmail.com.
